Misscarriage

A miscarriage, also known as a spontaneous abortion, is the loss of a pregnancy before the fetus can survive outside the womb. It typically occurs during the first 20 weeks of gestation, with the majority happening within the first trimester.

What Happens now?

Consider seeking support from a mental health professional. According to Reedy, experiencing a miscarriage is akin to losing a child.
